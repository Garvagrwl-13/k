Hurray! The moment is finally here. It's your English Exam. I understand how you are feeling right now.

Last minute advice:

  • Keep all stationary ready. Admit Card, exam board, pen, pencil, eraser, scale, sharpner, water bottle, etc.
  • Take enough sleep now.
  • Before exam, eat very light.
  • Eat a spoon of Dahi and Khaand(sugar/bura) before leaving home.
  • On the day of exam, Revise at home early morning. Avoid studying 1-2 hrs before exam.
  • Don't talk much before the exam. So, that you are in your headspace.
  • Take a deep breath.
  • Reach the exam centre on time. (Don't go late like you used to go to School)
    • It will cause panic if you reach late.
    • On reaching the centre, close your eyes and relax.
  • Avoid discussing paper after exam. Enjoy with friends. Motivate them. Forget about it and prepare for the next one.

The most important thing during any exam is that one must remain calm. Give your best. Being in a hurry and having a good speed are two different things. Drink water. Focus on your paper only. Read questions carefully. Utilise the question paper reading time.

All the best. Go smiling and leave smiling.
